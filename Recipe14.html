Paneer Pulao

Ingredients

  • To marinate Onions Green Chillies
    • Juice of one Lemon
    • 2 Green Chillies slit
    • 1 Piece of Onion
    • Salt
    • 1/4 tsp Sugar
  • For Rice
    • 1/2 cup Ghee
    • 1 Biryani Leaf
    • 4 Cardamoms
    • 4 Cloves
    • 1 Cinnamon
    • 1 tsp Black Cumin
    • Nutmeg â€“ A little
    • 1/2 tsp Pepper
    • 200 gm Paneer
    • 2 Onions strands
    • 1 tsp Ginger Garlic Paste
    • 1/4 tsp Turmeric
    • 1/2 tsp Roasted Cumin Powder
    • Salt
    • 1/2 cup Tomato Pieces
    • 1/2 cup Frozen Peas
    • 2 cups Basmati Rice soaked for an hour (1 cup = 185 gm)
    • 3 cups Water
    • 2 Green Chillies Pieces
    • Green Coriander Chopped - A little

Recipe Steps

  1. Add all the ingredients for marinating Onions and let it rest for an hour.
  2. Melt Ghee and add the dry spices.
  3. Add Paneer pieces and fry until light golden.
  4. In the remaining Ghee, add Onion strands and roast until light golden.
  5. Add Ginger Garlic paste, fry, and then add Turmeric, Salt, Cumin Powder, and some water.
  6. In the fried Spices, add Tomato Pieces and Peas and cook for 3 minutes.
  7. Then add the soaked Rice and fry until the moisture goes away.
  8. Add hot Water and then add Green Chillies, Green Coriander, and Paneer pieces. Cover and cook for two whistles on a high flame.
  9. Switch off the stove and let it rest for 20 minutes.
  10. After 20 minutes, strain the marinated Onions and Green Chillies pieces and spread them on the Rice.
  11. Serve with cool Raitha.
