Mutton Curry

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg Meat
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Ginger Paste
  • Salt
  • 1 tsp Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • 200 ml Onions Chopped
  • 1 cup Tomato paste
  • 3 tbsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 1/4 cup Coriander Powder
  • 3 Cardamoms
  • 5 Cloves
  • 2 Inches Cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp Pepper
  • 2 Biryani leaves
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1/3 cup Oil
  • Green Coriander â€“ a little
  • 4 Green Chillies
  • 250 ml Water

Recipe Steps

  1. Wash the Meat and coat it thoroughly with Ginger Garlic paste, Salt, Turmeric, Lemon juice, and Oil. Leave it in the fridge overnight or at least for an hour.
  2. Take the Meat out of the fridge, add the rest of the ingredients, and let the Spices get coated nicely by turning the Meat over again and again.
  3. Heat Oil in a cooker, add Meat and some Water, and cook until 5 whistles on a low flame. (Or cook until the Meat becomes soft).
  4. Once the steam is gone and the Meat gets cooked till soft, uncover the cooker and put it back on the stove.
  5. Add Green Coriander and cook for another 4-5 minutes.
  6. Remove from the fire, and there you have thick, spicy, aromatic Meat Curry ready.
